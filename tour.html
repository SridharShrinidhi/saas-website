Features

Our design tool helps the creative type with font customization

  • Typeset the mood

  • Track your type

  • Typeface-d

  • Font it

Typeset the mood

Our 4-step feature, with auto suggest, provides typefaces and combinations based on the mood of the article. Efficient typesetting with wide choices. Our users love this unique feature.

Track your typeface

Change the tracking, leading of your text for better readibility and flow. It also helps avoid orphans, widows for a balanced text. Now available as a 4 - step process.

Typeface-d

Combine and mix typefaces for fancy posters. Sans your serif and serif your sans for a creative typographic output. Typeface-d helps save time owing to its 4 stage approach.

Font it

Bring typeface characteristics from one typeface to another. Make light typefaces heavy, demi based on your typographic need. Tailored for every graphic designer, with an easy four- stage process.
